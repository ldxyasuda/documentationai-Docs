## Overview

L-DX empowers your apparel business with a cloud-based core system designed for digital transformation (DX). You face common challenges like stock stagnation, stockouts, and disjointed order-to-delivery processes. L-DX solves these by integrating inventory management, production tracking, and real-time order linkage. Connect it seamlessly to your ERP or existing systems for quick implementation and on-site adoption. Maximize profits through optimized stock levels and efficient operations.

## Quick Start

Get up and running with L-DX in minutes. Follow these steps to access your dashboard and configure basics.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Create your account at `https://dashboard.example.com/signup`. Use your business email for verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Systems" icon="plug">
    In the dashboard, navigate to Integrations. Add your ERP endpoint like `https://api.example.com/erp/sync`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import Data" icon="upload">
    Upload initial inventory via CSV or API. Sample endpoint: `POST https://api.example.com/v1/inventory/import`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor Live" icon="activity">
    View real-time dashboards for orders and stock. Set alerts for low inventory thresholds.
  </Step>
</Steps>
